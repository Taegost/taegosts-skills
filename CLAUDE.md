## Verifying Proposed Fixes

When acting on a proposed fix or worked example — from an issue, a PR review finding, or the user's own guidance — verify it actually produces the correct outcome before documenting or implementing it. A proposal drawn from one incident may generalize the wrong part of that incident. Don't transcribe proposed mechanics as guidance without checking them against the result they were meant to produce.
